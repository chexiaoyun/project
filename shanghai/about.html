ABOUT US

CRSA is a Joint Venture Company established by Sinotrans & CSC, Cambiaso Risso and WSICT , Wusongkou International Cruise Terminal, with the purpose of seeking for new challenges and opportunities to provide better service for Company.

The company has an international and experienced team who can provide all-around services like worldwide steamship agency, cruise agency,dry docking and new building, logistics and freight forwarding, etc.

The aim of CRSA is to be the most reliable partner for Company with our special-tailored and perfect services through continuous innovation and practices to make Company across every port in the world and realize infinite possibilities.
